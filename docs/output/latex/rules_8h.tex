int bot\+Play\+Card (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{\mbox{\hyperlink{struct_card}{Card}} \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}}]{player\+Deck}{}\end{DoxyParamCaption})}



Determines the card to be played by the bot. 

Decides which card the bot should play based on the current round suit and trump suit. If the round suit hasn\textquotesingle{}t been set, it sets the round suit to the suit of the first card in the bot\textquotesingle{}s deck and plays that card. If the bot has a card of the round suit, it plays that card. If the bot doesn\textquotesingle{}t have a card of the round suit, but has a card of the trump suit, it plays that card. If the bot doesn\textquotesingle{}t have a card of either the round suit or the trump suit, it plays the first card in its deck.
